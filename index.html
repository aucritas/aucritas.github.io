A game about free running

Explore the region of Marmocle! Progress through the zones and meet friendly animals as you learn about the world and how to overcome challenges in your path.

...paired with electronic music

The faster you go, the better the music gets.

There seem to be some neon-colored dungeons scattered around the region. Make sure to spot them and test your reflexes with rhythmic challenges.

Fech

Our clumsy protagonist is pink ferret who has yet to learn about the world. He's set on his first adventure through Marmocle, a peaceful region he suddenly found himself in.

Join a lovely community!

Fech The Ferret’s discord server is the core place to follow announcements and get to see behind the scenes of the development of the game .

Feel free to share your opinions and chat with devs, as well as meet other fans of the project!
